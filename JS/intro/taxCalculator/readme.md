## KENYA PAYE GUIDE

## FORMULA
**Gross Pay** (salary + allowances + taxable benefits)  
**(-) NSSF tier 1 & 2** (registered scheme only)
**(-)  Housing levy
**(=) Taxable Pay**  
**(×) Tax Bands** → **Gross Tax**  
**(-) Personal Relief** (KSh 2,400/month)  
**(=) NET PAYE** (deduct from employee, remit to KRA)
